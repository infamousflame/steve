Steve Lemay, LLC Provides More Than Tower Services:


Established in 2013 to provide top quality services for broadcast and communication towers across the world.  Our company provides personnel with extensive experience and training for an ever evolving industry.  Because of these high standards, we have developed a reputation of excellence in the execution of our services.

 

We are not limited to servicing towers.  As in many other industries, any job could easily encompass a much broader scope of needs for our clients.  We have experience including but not exclusive to tower maintenance, erection, analysis, and reinforcement.  We provide removal service for various types of towers. We have the knowledge to work on lighting systems, and antenna arrays. STEVE LEMAYLLC has vast experience with small and large projects. Our services also include complete build outs of cellular towers and microwave links, and comprehensive and detailed inspections as needed.

We implement safety measures at our jobsites that provide high measures of safety for all personnel involved insuring a quality of job performance that will serve your needs efficiently. Efficiency on the job equates to you as our clients meeting your financial goals.

 

Putting our team to work on your jobsite for repairs, construction, troubleshooting, maintenance or service provides you with a team of highly skilled, experienced professionals. We will find the solution you need with results that stand up to time.
