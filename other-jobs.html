Other Jobs of Significance of Steve Lemay

Seacomm Erectors was the General Contractor for the work listed below.

October 6th 2003- June 24th 2004 San Francisco, CA.  Did a voluntary seismic upgrade on Sutro Tower.  



May 27th 2003 – June 18th 2003 MN. Changed out the top guy wires on a 1500 foot tall tower and installed a side mount  antenna and run of 6” T/L for KXPM.


March 23rd 2003- May 8th 2003 Kansas City, MO. 1200’ Straight Stick with Elevator for Central Tower.  Also installed 4 side mounted antennas and T/L.


2000 – 2001 Nashville, TN.  Built the first ERI star mount  tower.  This was about 1200’ tall with a Marshall double car elevator.  


February 2000 Philadelphia, PA.  Built a 1200’ Kline Star Mount for American Tower and installed 7 antennas from various manufactures plus T/L.  This job was 8 months long and our contract was about 2.4 million.  Section weights were between 25K and 35K.  The number 5 wires were 3” diameter. After the tower was built we had to change out the #5B and #5C because of manufacturing defects. The #5C was laced through the adjacent tower guys.

January 1997- 2000         Built numerous cell towers, microwave links, inspections, and the like.
